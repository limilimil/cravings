French Toast recipe

Serving: 12-14

Total time: 1hr 5min

Prep time: 18min

Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (thick slices work best)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoom ground cinnamon
  • Pinch of salt
  • Butter or Cooking spray for greasing the pan
  • Optional toppings: maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh berries, whipped cream, etc.

Method

Step 1

Heat a non-stick skillet or griddle over medium heat.

Step 2

In a shallow dish, whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, and a pinch of salt. Make sure the mixture is well combined.

Step 3

Dip each slice of bread into the egg mixture, making sure both sides are coated evenly. Allow excess batter to drip off.

Step 4

Add a small amount of butter to the heated skillet or spray with cooking spray.Place the coated bread slices on the skillet and cook for 2-3 minutes on each side or until golden brown and cooked through.

Step 5

Remove the French toast from the skillet and place on a serving plate. Repeat the process for the remaining slices of bread.
