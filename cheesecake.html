Cheesecake recipe

Serving: 12-14

Total time: 1hr 5min

Prep time: 18min

Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 250g digestive biscuits
  • 100g butter, meltedr
  • 1 vanilla pod
  • 600g full fat soft cheese
  • 100g icing sugar
  • 284ml pot of double cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter or cooking oil

Method

Step 1:

To make the base, butter and line a 23cm loose-bottomed tin with baking parchment.

Step 2:

Put the digestive biscuits in a plastic food bag and crush to crumbs using a rolling pin.

Step 3:

Transfer the crumbs to a bowl, then pour over the melted butter. Mix thoroughly until the crumbs are completely coated.

Step 4:

Slice the vanilla pod in half lengthways, leaving the tip intact, so that the two halves are still joined. Holding onto the tip of the pod, scrape out the seeds using the back of a kitchen knife.

Step 5:

Pour the double cream into a bowl and whisk with an electric mixer until it’s just starting to thicken to soft peaks.
