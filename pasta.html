Pasta Recipe

Serving: 2

Total time: 28 mins

Prep time: 2 mins

Cooking time: 25 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • ½ onion
  • 3 garlic cloves
  • ¼ tsp chilli flakes
  • 100g tomato purée
  • 100ml double cream
  • 200g penne or rigatoni pasta
  • small handful of basil leaves, to serve
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter or cooking oil

Method

Step 1:

Heat the oil in a large frying pan

Step 2:

Add the shallot and a large pinch of salt and gently fry over a low heat for 10 mins

Step 3:

Add the garlic and chilli flakes and cook for 30 seconds. Stir through the tomato purée, cook for 2 mins, then stir through the vodka and cook for 3 mins.

Step 4:

Cook the pasta in salted water following pack instructions. Drain and reserve 150ml cooking water.

Step 5:

Add roughly 50ml of the water to the tomato sauce, then tip in the pasta and cheese
