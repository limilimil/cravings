Omelette

Serving: 12-14

Total time: 1hr 5min

Prep time: 18min

Cooking time: 15min

Ingredients

  • 500g potatoes
  • 1 white onion, preferrably white
  • 150ml extra-virgin olive oil
  • 3 tbsp chopped flat-leaf parsley
  • 6 eggs

Method

Step 1

Scrape the new potatoes or leave the skins on, if you prefer. Cut them into thick slices. Chop the onion.

Step 2

Heat the extra-virgin olive oil in a large frying pan, add the potatoes and onion and stew gently, partially covered, for 30 mins, stirring occasionally until the potatoes are softened.

Step 3

Strain the potatoes and onion through a colander into a large bowl (set the strained oil aside).

Beat the eggs separately, then stir into the potatoes with the parsley and plenty of salt and pepper. Heat a little of the strained oil in a smaller pan.

Step 4

Tip everything into the pan and cook on a moderate heat, using a spatula to shape the omelette into a cushion.
